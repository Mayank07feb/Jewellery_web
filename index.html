About Us

We are one of the oldest business families in India with a family legacy of 112 years in business. Our journey began with a vision to contribute to nation-building and self-sustainability in pre-independent India. Our core values of ethics, honesty, and transparency have been the cornerstone of our group.

In 1993, we ventured into jewellery retailing in Thrissur, Kerala, India. Today, with 137 showrooms across India and the GCC region, we continue our legacy of excellence.
